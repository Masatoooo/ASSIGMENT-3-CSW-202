About SavePlate

Turning food habits into meaningful action

SavePlate is a food management platform built for households who care about reducing waste. We believe the kitchen is where small daily choices create the biggest environmental impact.

By tracking what you have, planning what you'll eat, and sharing what you can't — SavePlate turns everyday meals into acts of mindfulness and community care.

How It Works

Three steps to zero waste

SavePlate fits naturally into your daily routine — no complicated setup required.

01
📦
Add Your Inventory

Log your food items — name, quantity, expiry date, and storage location. SavePlate keeps everything organized and sends you alerts before things go bad.

02
📅
Plan Your Weekly Meals

Use the weekly meal planner to assign your existing ingredients to meals. Get AI-powered recipe suggestions that prioritize items closest to expiry.

03
🤝
Donate the Surplus

Can't use it in time? List surplus food for donation with one click. Connect with people in your area who can make use of what you can't.

Our Mission

A world where no good food goes to waste

We're building technology that makes sustainable living simple, social, and rewarding — one plate at a time.

  • Reduce household food waste through smart tracking and alerts
  • Build a local food-sharing community through our donation network
  • Help families save money by making better use of what they already have
  • Contribute to a measurable reduction in food-related carbon emissions
